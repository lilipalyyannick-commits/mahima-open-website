FAQ

How many matches will my team play?
Every team is guaranteed a minimum of 2 matches. Win your main draw matches and you could play up to 4. Full consolation brackets mean even first-round losers continue competing.
What does "golden point" mean?
At deuce (40-40), the receiving team chooses which side to receive, and the next point wins the game. No advantage — every deuce is decisive. This keeps matches within time limits and adds excitement.
Can I play both padel and tennis?
No, you can only register for either padel or tennis. Choose the sport you want to compete in and give it your full energy.
Is there parking and food available?
Mahima offers ample parking, along with a café and bar area on-site. Wellness facilities including a sauna, ice bath, and pool are available to all participants, and an in-house physiotherapist is also present for support and recovery.
What should I bring?
Your racket, proper sport shoes, comfortable sportswear, and a water bottle. Mahima provides towels and tournament balls.
What do the champions and players take home?
There's a prize pool of IDR 50,000,000 to be divided across the different categories, consisting of rackets, merchandise, vouchers, lessons, and more. All participants will receive something.
Is there physiotherapy available during the tournament?
Yes, we have standby physiotherapy on-site throughout the tournament for injury prevention, treatment, and recovery support.
